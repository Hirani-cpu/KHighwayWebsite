Our Story

K Highway DIY Hardware Shop Shop was founded with a simple mission: to make quality window and door hardware accessible to everyone. Whether you're a homeowner tackling a DIY project, a tradesperson on a job, or a property manager maintaining multiple buildings, we're here to help you find exactly what you need.

We supply a focused range of window and door hardware items suitable for common residential and commercial installations. Our aim is to make it easy for you to find the parts you need, with clear descriptions and straightforward ordering.

Based in the UK, we pride ourselves on fast shipping, competitive prices, and excellent customer service. Every product we sell is carefully selected to ensure quality and durability.
