What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept payments via PayPal, which includes:
  • PayPal balance
  • Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • Debit cards
  • Pay Later options through PayPal
All payments are processed securely through PayPal's encrypted payment system.
How can I track my order? +
Once your order has been dispatched, you'll receive an email with your tracking number and a link to track your parcel on the Royal Mail website. You can also view your order status by logging into your account on our website.
Can I cancel or modify my order? +
If you need to cancel or modify your order, please contact us as soon as possible. If your order hasn't been dispatched yet, we can usually make changes. Once an order has been shipped, you'll need to wait for delivery and then request a return if needed.
Do you offer bulk discounts? +
Yes! For large orders or trade customers, we offer competitive bulk pricing. Please contact us with your requirements and we'll provide a custom quote.

How long will delivery take? +
Orders placed before 2pm on working days are typically dispatched the same day. Delivery times depend on your chosen shipping method - Royal Mail Tracked 24 arrives within 1-2 working days, while Tracked 48 takes 2-3 working days.
Do you deliver internationally? +
Currently, we deliver to the United Kingdom and Ireland. For international deliveries outside of these areas, please contact us for a quote and available options.

üîÑ Returns & Refunds

What is your returns policy? +
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return any unused item in its original packaging within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. See our Returns page for full details.
How do I return an item? +
To return an item:
  • Contact us with your order number and reason for return
  • We'll send you a return authorisation within 24 hours
  • Pack the item securely and send it back using a tracked service
  • Receive your refund within 5-7 working days of us receiving the item
What if my item arrives damaged? +
If your item arrives damaged, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the damage. We'll arrange a free collection and send a replacement or full refund immediately. Please keep all original packaging until the issue is resolved.

Many of our products come with installation instructions. For more complex installations, we recommend consulting a professional. If you need guidance, feel free to contact us and we'll do our best to help.
